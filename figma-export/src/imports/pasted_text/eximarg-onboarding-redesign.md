## One recommendation that will make EXIMARG feel much more premium

I would **not** make "Start Journey" go directly to a registration form.

Instead, I'd create a **2–3 screen onboarding prologue** before authentication:

1. **Welcome to EXIMARG** – "Let's build your export business."
2. **Choose your path** – "New Exporter" or "Experienced Exporter."
3. **Sign In / Create Account** – only now ask for authentication.

This keeps the emotional momentum from the landing page and makes the first interaction feel like the beginning of a guided experience rather than an abrupt form. It also reinforces your core product idea of **Duolingo for exporters**, where the journey starts before the account creation.
