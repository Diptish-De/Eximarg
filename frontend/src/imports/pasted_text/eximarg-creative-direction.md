# One thing I would change in your vision

This is the only thing I disagree with from earlier conversations:

**Don't make the landing page too "export-themed."**

If you fill it with:

* Cargo ships
* Containers
* Ports
* Documents
* Maps
* Invoices

…it can quickly feel like a logistics company rather than a premium technology brand.

Instead, use those elements as **subtle metaphors**. Let typography, spacing, motion, and storytelling carry most of the emotional weight. The export visuals should support the narrative—not dominate it.

That's the difference between a memorable product experience and a themed website.
