🗺️ Export Passport
The single most iconic idea. Instead of a dashboard, give every exporter a digital passport — a beautifully designed booklet that fills with rubber stamps as they export to new countries. Germany gets a specific stamp design, Japan gets another. Each stamp has the product name, date, and order value. It becomes a collectible artifact of their export journey. Buyers get verified "visa" pages. After 10 countries, a special "World Traveller" foil stamp appears.

Replace/supplement XP levels with specific, named achievements:

📜 Export Rank & Titles
Instead of "Level 6 Bronze", give exporters actual titles that evolve:

🌱 Local Seller       (Level 1–2)
🧭 Emerging Exporter  (Level 3–4)
⚓ Trade Navigator    (Level 5–6)
🌐 Global Merchant    (Level 7–8)

Show the title prominently in the sidebar, profile dropdown, and dashboard greeting. When a title changes, a full-screen celebration plays for 3 seconds.

🎯 Active Quest System
A "Today's Quests" card on the dashboard with 2–3 rotating missions:

Daily:

Reply to a buyer inquiry → +50 XP
Upload one document → +30 XP
Update a product price → +20 XP
Weekly:

Add 3 new products → +300 XP + badge
Reach out to 5 new buyers → +200 XP
Complete a shipment → +500 XP
One-time:

First export to UAE → "Gulf Pioneer" badge
Invoice over $10,000 → "Big Deal" badge
Quests have a progress bar that fills in real time and a satisfying completion pop when done.

🔥 Streak System
Track consecutive days of meaningful activity (not just login):

Responded to a buyer
Added/updated a product
Sent an invoice
Uploaded a document
Show a flame counter: "🔥 12-day active streak". At 7 days → bonus XP multiplier. At 30 days → exclusive "Consistent Exporter" badge. Break the streak → get a "Last chance to save your streak" push notification.

🧠 Skill Tree
Let exporters choose a specialization path that unlocks different features:

          [Core Exporter]
          /      |      \
    Food &    Textiles   Machinery
    Agri      & Crafts   & Chemicals

Each path unlocks:
- Specific AI templates
- Category-specific buyer pool
- Specialized compliance docs
- Niche market insights
Choosing a path makes the product feel personal and removes overwhelm.

📈 Trust Score (visible, growing)
A dynamic score from 0–1000 that updates in real time based on:

Documents verified (+points)
Buyer reviews (+points)
Completed shipments (+points)
Response time to buyers (+/−)
Compliance up to date (+/−)
Displayed as a prominent number with a sparkline trend chart. Buyers can see your score when browsing your catalogue. "Your Trust Score increased by 8 points today" feels amazing.

🗺️ Country Conquest Map
The global footprint map turns into a conquest visualization:

Countries you've exported to are shaded in your brand color
Countries you have active buyer conversations in pulse gently
Countries where you have pending inquiries show as outlines
Hover over a country to see: shipments, revenue, active buyers
Watching your map fill up becomes addictive.

⚔️ Season-Based Battle Pass
Every quarter, EXIMARG runs an Export Season:

Season 1: "The Spice Route" (focus on food exports to Europe)

Free track: 10 milestones, basic rewards Premium track: 15 milestones, unlock premium features + exclusive badges

🎰 Daily Trade Brief + Spin
Every morning, a card appears:

"Good morning Rajesh. Your Basmati Rice got 3 new views from Hamburg overnight. Complete today's challenge to earn 2× XP until 6 PM."

With a daily spin wheel (not random gambling — always some XP or a useful power-up like "Featured for 24 hours"). The spin makes opening the app exciting.

Show exporters where they rank:
